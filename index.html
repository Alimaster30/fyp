Innovation

The Challenge

Thousands of Calls

Universities receive overwhelming volume of repetitive inquiries daily.

Wasted Resources

Human operators spend valuable time answering common questions.

Language Barriers

Limited Urdu support reduces service quality for many students.

LLM Limitations

LLM-based systems are slow, expensive, and prone to hallucinations.

Our Solution

Introducing the AI Voice Agent - a purpose-built system using intent classification instead of LLMs for faster, more reliable responses.

Lightning Fast

10x faster response time than LLM-based systems

Verified Answers

Predefined, accurate responses with no hallucinations

Smart Escalation

Seamless handoff to human operators when needed

How It Works

Voice Input

Student speaks query in Urdu or English

Speech to Text

Convert speech to text using Whisper

Intent Classification

Classify query intent using ML models

Response Retrieval

Fetch verified response from database

Voice Output

Convert text to speech for response

Human Transfer

Escalate on low confidence scores

Use Cases

Admissions Queries

Query:

"میں بی ایس سی میں ایڈمشن لینا چاہتا ہوں، آخری تاریخ کیا ہے؟"

Response:

"بی ایس سی میں ایڈمشن کی آخری تاریخ 15 اگست ہے۔ آن لائن فارم بھرنے کے لیے ہماری ویب سائٹ پر جائیں۔"

Fee Structure

Query:

"What is the fee for first semester computer science?"

Response:

"First semester fee for computer science is Rs. 45,000 including tuition and lab fees."

Programs & Departments

Query:

"کس کالج میں انجینئرنگ کی ڈگری ہے؟"

Response:

"انجینئرنگ کی ڈگریز انجینئرنگ فیکلٹی میں دستیاب ہیں، جس میں سافٹ ویئر، مکینیکل اور سول انجینئرنگ شامل ہیں۔"

Exams & Results

Query:

"When will midterm results be announced?"

Response:

"Midterm results will be announced on December 15th. You can check them on the student portal."

Hostel & Transport

Query:

"ہوسٹل میں سیٹ حاصل کرنے کی عمل کیا ہے؟"

Response:

"ہوسٹل سیٹ کے لیے ایڈمشن آفس میں فارم جمع کروائیں۔ ہوسٹل فی Rs. 8,000 ماہانہ ہے۔"

Document Requirements

Query:

"What documents are needed for admission?"

Response:

"Required documents: Matric certificate, CNIC/B-form, father's CNIC, 4 passport size photos, and domicile."
